Intro to this brain storm

In this session, our coding team will explore strategies to enhance productivity and foster innovation. We'll discuss the integration of AI tools like ChatGPT and Notion to streamline routine tasks, improve project management, and assist in rapid prototyping. Additionally, we'll examine the implementation of a four-day workweek to maintain high performance while preventing burnout. The goal is to identify actionable steps that leverage technology and work-life balance to boost efficiency and creativity within our team

The contents that will be included in the meeting

  1. Project Progress Update Meeting:
    • Review of items discussed in the last meeting.
    • Report on the current project's progress.
    • Discussion of challenges and bottlenecks encountered.
    • Set goals and plans for the next phase.
    • Resource requirements and allocation status.
  2. Team Building Meeting:
    • Feedback on team members' performance.
    • Issues and suggestions for improving team collaboration.
    • Team activities and building plans.
    • Identifying training and skill development needs.
    • Discussion on work-life balance for team members.
  3. Brainstorming Meeting:
    • Identify the core issue or goal to discuss.
    • Encourage the proposal of creative ideas and new solutions.
    • Evaluate the feasibility of different options.
    • Summarize and categorize the ideas presented.
    • Determine the next steps and action plans.
